Data Science Research and Development Intern

Summary

I interned at CCC Intelligent Solutions Inc headquartered at Chicago as a Data Science R&D intern from June 2022 to April 2023 where I reported to my manager Francisco Munoz Soler.

Experience

During my eleven-month internship as a Data Science R&D intern, I had the opportunity to work on a variety of large-scale industry projects. My responsibilities primarily revolved around building custom transformer architectures, establishing continuous development pipelines for model training and evaluation, and creating visualizations to showcase model performance. Throughout the internship, I utilized Python, PyTorch, Kubernetes, PyTorch-lightning, Jupyter Notebook, VS Code, and GitLab.

Working alongside experienced professionals, I gained insights into bridging the gap between technical expertise and commercial perspectives. Additionally, I participated in a 3-day hackathon, refining my collaboration skills and ability to meet tight deadlines. Among my notable achievements, I engineered a pipeline for multi-label multi-class image classification. By leveraging a Vision Transformer (ViT), I achieved an impressive accuracy of 97.8% across 100,000 instances. Furthermore, I developed custom training and evaluation pipelines to ensure efficient model development.

To demonstrate correlations among vehicle components, I created compelling 3D visualizations using TensorBoard, reducing the dimensionality of a 700+ dimensional embedding. These visualizations effectively highlighted the relationships between damaged parts. Moreover, I gained hands-on experience in deploying jobs on V100 GPUs using Kubernetes in a distributed manner. PyTorch Distributed Data Parallel (DDP) played a vital role in enabling efficient parallel processing.

In summary, my internship at CCC Intelligent Solutions provided invaluable exposure to industry practices, enhancing my skills as a Computer Science professional. It also taught me how to effectively manage academic commitments alongside professional responsibilities. Through the creation of training and evaluation pipelines, I developed a holistic understanding of the model development process. The 3D visualizations I created using TensorBoard showcased the insights gained from complex data. Overall, this experience has been instrumental in my growth as a Data Science professional.
