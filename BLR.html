Associate Data Analyst

Experience


In a dynamic role at BLR Logistiks, I embarked on a journey as an Associate Data Analyst, navigating the challenges and opportunities of a logistics environment amidst the backdrop of the COVID-19 pandemic. Leveraging my skills in Spark, Python, and SQL, I delved into the realm of data analysis, focusing on fundamental tasks essential for optimizing logistics operations. For instance, I conducted data aggregation operations using Spark to process vast volumes of shipment and inventory data. By applying Python scripts, I streamlined data workflows, reducing processing time by 25% and improving efficiency in handling logistics datasets. SQL queries were instrumental in accessing and analyzing data, enabling me to extract key insights regarding shipment delivery times, inventory turnover rates, and customer feedback metrics.


To tackle the sales prediction challenge, I initially applied multivariate nonlinear regression. However, I soon recognized the potential of neural networks in capturing the intricate patterns within the sales data. This motivated me to explore further and experiment with long short-term memory (LSTM) networks, which are particularly suitable for time series data analysis. Implementing LSTM allowed me to incorporate the temporal aspect of the sales data and achieve more accurate predictions. Amidst the uncertainties brought about by the pandemic, my role as an Associate Data Analyst became even more crucial. I collaborated with cross-functional teams to develop actionable insights that helped mitigate supply chain disruptions and adapt to evolving market demands. Through interactive Power BI dashboards, stakeholders gained visibility into critical logistics metrics, such as shipment delays, inventory levels, and customer satisfaction scores.


As a part-time team member, I embraced the opportunity to learn and grow in a dynamic industry, contributing my expertise to drive operational excellence and enhance customer service. Despite the challenges posed by remote work and fluctuating demand, I remained resilient, adapting to new ways of working and leveraging data-driven approaches to overcome logistical challenges. In summary, my experience as an Associate Data Analyst at BLR Logistiks was characterized by a blend of learning, innovation, and resilience. Through my contributions in data analysis and insights generation, I played a vital role in supporting the company's mission of delivering efficient third-party logistics services amidst unprecedented times.
