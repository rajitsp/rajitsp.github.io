Hey! I'm Rajit Puzhakkarezhath

I am captivated by the possibilities of artificial intelligence and machine learning, constantly staying updated with the latest advancements and breakthroughs.


Drawing inspiration from the captivating storytelling of Christopher Nolan's movies, such as "Interstellar" and "The Dark Knight," I find myself deeply immersed in narratives that challenge conventional thinking and explore the depths of human experience. Known for my "wicked" smash, I thrive on the competitive spirit of the game and the strategic thinking it requires. Badminton has taught me valuable lessons in discipline, teamwork, and perseverance.


Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I cherish the company of friends and the joy that comes from shared experiences and deep conversations. It is in these moments that lasting memories are created and relationships are nurtured. I am deeply grateful to my family, who has sacrificed so much for me. Their unwavering support and belief in my dreams have been instrumental in shaping who I am today. Their sacrifices have given me the opportunity to pursue my education, explore my passions, and strive for excellence. I also have love for music, with artists like Bruno Mars, Charlie Puth, and Anuv Jain being among my favorites. Exploring new gadgets, especially headphones and mobile phones, fascinates me. These interests add vibrancy to my life and inspire me to appreciate the beauty in different forms of art and technology


As I embark on my journey in the field of AI and data science, my goal is to contribute to the development of intelligent systems that drive innovation, solve complex problems, and make a positive impact on society. Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me. I look forward to connecting and discovering more about you too! Together, let us embrace the challenges and awaken to the beauty that lies within them.
