Data Science Intern

Experience


During my internship from June to July 2019, I had the opportunity to work under Garima Pandey at the Ultratech Cement division of Aditya Birla Group. As my first internship experience, it was a transformative period of learning and growth for me. My primary task during the internship was to predict the sales of a specific region, plant, and type of cement. At the beginning of the internship, I had just started familiarizing myself with machine learning, specifically linear regression. However, as the internship progressed, I was able to delve into more complex techniques and models, such as neural networks.


To tackle the sales prediction challenge, I initially applied multivariate nonlinear regression. However, I soon recognized the potential of neural networks in capturing the intricate patterns within the sales data. This motivated me to explore further and experiment with long short-term memory (LSTM) networks, which are particularly suitable for time series data analysis. Implementing LSTM allowed me to incorporate the temporal aspect of the sales data and achieve more accurate predictions. Working with industry data was an eye-opening experience for me. I gained hands-on experience in data cleaning and preprocessing, which involved dealing with real-world data challenges and ensuring data quality. This experience deepened my understanding of the importance of data preparation in achieving reliable and meaningful results.


Throughout the internship, I was fortunate to receive guidance from my supervisor, Garima Pandey, who provided valuable insights and expertise in the field of machine learning and data analysis. Collaborating with her and other team members was an enriching experience that enhanced my teamwork and communication skills. In just a short period, I acquired substantial knowledge in machine learning techniques, specifically applying them to solve real-world business problems in the cement industry. This internship allowed me to witness the practical application of my academic learnings and gave me a taste of the challenges and rewards of working in a professional setting.

Overall, my internship experience at Aditya Birla Group's Ultratech Cement division was truly amazing. It provided me with a strong foundation in machine learning, data analysis, and working with industry data. The skills and knowledge I gained during this internship continue to be invaluable as I pursue my career in the field.
